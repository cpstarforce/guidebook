A Welcome Letter

This article is intended for newly inducted staff members.

Welcome to the team! We are excited to have you on board and look forward to working with you. As a new member of our staff team, you will play a vital role in helping us achieve our goals and serve the army effectively.

About Your Position

Your position entails the following broad responsibilities…

  • Keeping SF chats active
  • Ensuring safety in SF and moderating members
  • Helping recruits and other members
  • Participating in administrative or moderator discussions
  • Be fully informed and transform into an open book

As you enter the team, you might find a gradual shift in your routine. That's completely normal! Get ready as you might be asked questions by members or be reached out for help and support. It is important to not be clueless at the time of answering questions. You are expected to be well-informed about the army and its rules. If you are unsure about something, please do not hesitate to ask your fellow staff members or higher ranks for assistance. But, don't just neglect the question and leave it unanswered. We are all here to help each other, and we want to ensure that you feel comfortable performing your duty.

Depending on your role, you may have additional responsibilities at hand. For example, if you are a High Command member, you are responsible for scheduling events and participating in high-profile decision making.

Staff Structure

The staff team is structured in a hierarchy. Staff ranks are divided into 3 rank groups — the (Junior) Staff, the High Command ("HCOM" or "Senior Staff"), and the Leadership.
