Hello there, fellow agent. Welcome to the official guidebook for everything Star Force. Reading through this foreword is not really necessary, so you can skip this. This is an introductory article that helps you understand the guidebook and how to use it.

About the Guidebook

This guidebook began being written in late May 2025, and received a brief overhaul in August 2025. It is a comprehensive guide to Club Penguin Armies, and includes all the secrets, tips, and details you need to know to become an excellent staff member. The guidebook is divided into several sections, each covering a different aspect of the army. You can navigate through the guidebook using the sidebar on the left side of the screen.

The guidebook is designed to be easy to read and understand. Every article includes clear explanations, examples, and images to help you progress. The guidebook is also regularly updated to ensure that it remains relevant and accurate. Remember, if you find any errors or discover outdated information, please don't hesitate to contact the Leadership. The guidebook is a living document, and we encourage you to contribute to it. If you have any ideas or suggestions for new articles, please let us know. We are always looking for ways to improve the guidebook and make it even more useful for our members.

Happy reading!
